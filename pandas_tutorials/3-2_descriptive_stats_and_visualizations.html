5.2 Descriptive Statistics and Data Visualizations¶

The five most important visualizations are

  1. histogram
  2. boxplot
  3. Kernel Density Estimation
  4. violin plot
  5. Cumulative Distribution Function

The Seaborn library is the best plotting library ever!

Cumulative Distribution Function (CDF)¶

In [ ]:
A quick way of looking at percentiles.

Use:
seaborn.kdeplot( val = , cumulative = True)

In [75]:
# CDF shows the probability that a random value from the population is greater or less than a CDF bin

# Ex: there is an 80% probability that a random titanic passenger is YOUNGER than 40

seaborn.kdeplot(titanic.Age.dropna(), cumulative=True)
Out[75]:
<matplotlib.axes._subplots.AxesSubplot at 0x7fc01fe6f510>
